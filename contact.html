Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a price quote for a project?

Please use the contact form above to describe your project scope (e.g., number of pages, custom design needed, specific media assets). I will review your request and send a detailed quote within 1-2 business days.

Can you handle both the design and the coding for my website?

Yes, absolutely. My unique strength is combining my skills as a Web Developer (HTML, CSS, JS) and Graphic Designer (Photoshop, Canva) to ensure the final product is both beautiful and functional. See the Services page for my hybrid packages.

What software do you use for graphic design and video editing?

I primarily use Adobe Photoshop for professional image manipulation, Canva for efficient marketing assets, and Filmora for high-quality video editing and production.

Do you offer revisions after the project is complete?

Yes, all projects include a defined number of revisions during the development phase. Post-launch support and maintenance plans are also available; please see the Services page for details on long-term support.
